About the Conference

The IEEE JSS STU ICETEG 2025 aims to provide a platform for researchers, scholars, and industry leaders to share their ideas on emerging technologies in electronics and green energy. The conference will focus on a wide range of topics including AI, green energy, and IoT.

Conference Tracks

Nanotechnology Applications

Advancements and innovations in nanotechnology for various applications.

Communication and Networking

Latest trends in communication systems and networking technologies.

Green Energy

Explore sustainable energy solutions and their impact on the environment.

Computing Technology

Innovations in computing technology and its applications in various fields.

Biomedical Applications

Technological advancements in the field of biomedicine and healthcare.
